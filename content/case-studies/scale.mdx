Scale AI, the data infrastructure company powering the AI revolution, operates in a world where every meeting is extremely high stakes. With deals potentially worth tens of millions, there's no room for being underprepared.

## The Challenge

Scale's enterprise team faces unique pressure:

- Every meeting could be worth tens of millions of dollars
- Complex account contexts spanning multiple stakeholders and initiatives
- The need to validate strategy before every high-stakes conversation

## The Endgame Approach

Scale uses Endgame to ensure complete preparation:

1. **Synthesize all account context** from every available source
2. **Validate strategy** against current account intelligence
3. **Brief the team** with comprehensive, accurate information before every meeting

## The Results

With Endgame, Scale's enterprise team never walks into a meeting unprepared:

- Complete synthesis of account context before every meeting
- Strategy validation that gives confidence in the approach
- Critical preparation for deals worth tens of millions
